London Lua

London Lua is a user group for all aspects of the Lua programming language.

Meetings will take place monthly at Skills Matter in Clerkenwell, London.

Meetings are on the first Thursday of the month. The next meeting is 6 December 2012 at 18:30.

From a software engineering background, he now spends most of his time in the browser, coding JavaScript. Participation is open to everyone, whether a beginner or experienced, about all aspects of Lua, whether for scripting, games, mobile, web, embedded or other uses, the whole range of uses.
