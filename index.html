London Lua

London Lua is a user group for all aspects of the Lua programming language.

Meetings will take place monthly at Skills Matter in Clerkenwell, London.

Meetings are on the first Thursday of the month. The next meeting is 2 August 2012. Topic to be announced very shortly.

Past meetings:

  • Scripting Nginx with Lua, by James Hurst

Participation is open to everyone, whether a beginner or experienced, about all aspects of Lua, whether for scripting, games, mobile, web, embedded or other uses, the whole range of uses. If you would like to give a talk, sponsor the group, or suggest a topic, please mail me at justin@londonlua.org, or use the LondonLua wiki page. Talks should be about technical subjects, around the use of Lua, and will all be available on video afterwards.

We are on twitter as @londonlua.

We have a mailing list hosted at freelists, please subscribe to keep up to date with what is going on or ask questions.
